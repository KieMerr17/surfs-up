What We Offer


Group Beginner:
Group size of maximum 6 students to 1 instructor. This is designed for people with little to no experience and looking to learn to surf or improve their basic skills further.


Group Improver:
Group size of maximum 4 students to 1 instructor. This is for surfers who already know the basic technique, can catch broken waves and are looking to develop their skills further onto unbroken 'green' waves.


Private 1-2-1:
We give you your own instructor and you can focus soley on your individual requirements, from basic skills up to advanced techniques, the choice is yours.
