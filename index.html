About Us

We are a family run business based in Siargao, Philippines. With over 20 years experience working in the surfing industry, this has grown to become the no.1 place to come to for surfing lessons whilst in Siargao.


We opened the School in 1994 following working for another company because we decided we could give our students a much better experience, and we have never looked back!


We have grown each year since, and now employ experienced instructors in all our sports and operate in multiple sites along the beautiful coastline of Siargao.


Adam is a senior finalist in 2017.

Mike was also finalist in the European Championships 2019.


Community and the environment are central to our vision for the next decade. We have done our first carbon footprint audit and put in place strategies to work towards net zero.

What We Offer

  • Group Beginner:

    Group size of maximum 6 students to 1 instructor. This is designed for people with little to no experience and looking to learn to surf or improve their basic skills further.
    After learning the basic technique on the beautiful sand beach, you will take those skills with you into the water and use them to ride your first wave.

  • Group Improver:

    Group size of maximum 4 students to 1 instructor. This is for surfers with some experience with basic surfing techniques.
    You can catch broken waves and looking to progress your skills further onto unbroken (green) waves, with scope to learn left and right turns.

  • Private 1-2-1:

    We give you your own instructor and you can focus soley on your individual requirements, from a private beginner lesson learing basic surfing skills up to advanced techniques to use on the waves, the choice is yours.
